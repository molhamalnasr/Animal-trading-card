Red panda

Red panda mostly eats bamboo, but it also consumes fruit, roots, eggs and small mammals, red panda has additional thumb which is used for grabbing of the bamboo sticks.

  • scientific name: Ailurus fulgens
  • average length: 58 Cm
  • average lifespan: 12 to 14 years
  • habitat: high-altitude temperate forests

The red panda has reddish brown fur with distinct white marks on its face and tail. It has a very long fluffy tail and small ears. Its whiskers are white and long.
